## 🖼️ Image Specifications

### **Service Card Images**
- Size: 800x600px minimum
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3
- Quality: High (80-90% compression)

### **Gallery Images**
- Size: 1200x900px minimum
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Aspect Ratio: Flexible (cropped to fit)
- Quality: High

### **Video Thumbnail**
- Size: 1920x1080px (16:9)
- Format: JPG
- Shows most impressive project or team photo

### **Before/After Images**
- Size: 800x600px each (matching pair)
- Format: JPG
- Must be same angle/perspective
- Lighting should be consistent

## 🔧 How to Replace Images

### **Method 1: Keep Unsplash URLs (Quick Fix)**
Images load from Unsplash CDN - fast and free but not your actual work.

### **Method 2: Replace with Local Files (Recommended)**

1. Save your photos in a new folder: `/reviews/images/`

2. Update HTML image tags:
```html
<!-- Old -->
<img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-..." alt="...">

<!-- New -->
<img src="images/patio-project-1.jpg" alt="Modern patio installation Coventry">
```

3. Optimize images before uploading:
   - Use TinyPNG.com or similar to compress
   - Target file size: 100-300KB per image
   - Maintains quality while improving load speed
